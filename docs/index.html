General

  • You must have turned 18 by the time contributor sign ups end to participate.
  • You are not required to have previous zine experience.
  • You must have a Discord account/create a Discord account.
  • You may not share screenshots and/or content from the Discord server.
  • Team ups between writers and artists are permitted.
  • Deadlines are expected to be met accordingly. If for any reason, an extension is needed, you must communicate with the mod team.
  • This is a SFW zine. NSFW, dead dove, and problematic content will not be featured in the zine.
  • AUs, genderbends, and canon compliant content is permitted.

Writers

  • One fully completed fic with a maximum word count of 2.5k, with a 100-word leeway below and a 500-leeway above.
  • KiriTodo must be a romantic main pairing.
  • Love languages must be relevant to the fic. You are not limited to the five most popular love languages.

Artists

  • For page artists: One single or spread, full page illustration. Must be fully rendered.
  • For merch artists: Two or more pieces minimum.
  • Love languages must be relevant to your pieces. You are not limited to the five most popular love languages.
